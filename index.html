About Me


I'm a self taught web designer, developer, co-founder and entrepreneur based in Finland. I'm currently part of a small VR(Virtual Reality) development team in an upcoming start-up called Virtual-Dawn, developing awesome VR games. Competences: Languages and Frameworks: Javascript, C#m HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, Bootstrap3, Angular.js, Meteor.js. Tools & expertise: Git, Responsive Web Design, Agile Methodologies, Unity5 3D, Photoshop, VR Level Design, and Unity Generalist.
